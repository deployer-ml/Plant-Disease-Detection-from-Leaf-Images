Why Detect Plant Diseases?

Plant diseases significantly impact growth and yield. Early detection through AI helps:

Prevent Plant Diseases
  1. Follow good sanitation practices
  2. Fertilize to maintain plant health
  3. Inspect new plants before introducing them
  4. Allow soil to warm before planting
  5. Rotate crops annually
  6. Ensure proper air circulation
  7. Promptly remove diseased foliage
